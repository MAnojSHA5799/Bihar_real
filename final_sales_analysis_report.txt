
================ REAL ESTATE SALES ANALYSIS REPORT ================

Total Positive Reviews: 13
Total Negative Reviews: 10

THEORETICAL BASIS:

1. Trust-Based Purchase Theory:
Real estate is a high-value decision. Buyers require legal clarity,
transparent pricing, and timely possession. Financial complaints and
refund disputes increase perceived risk and reduce purchase intention.

2. Financial Risk & Loss Aversion:
Middle-income buyers depend on home loans. Project delays create EMI
burden and financial stress. Hidden charges reduce trust and negatively
impact word-of-mouth reputation.

3. SERVQUAL Service Quality Model:
Sales improve when reliability, responsiveness, assurance, and
construction quality are maintained. Delays and poor workmanship
reduce reliability perception.

ROOT CAUSES OF SALES DECLINE:
- Project delays
- Incomplete work
- Refund disputes
- Extra hidden charges
- Damp walls & quality issues

KEY DRIVERS OF SALES GROWTH:
- Value for money pricing
- Professional team behavior
- Eco-friendly branding
- High-quality materials
- Strong location advantage

CONCLUSION:
Sales probability directly depends on Trust Score and Risk Score.
Reducing financial disputes, improving construction quality,
and ensuring transparent communication can significantly
increase future sales performance.
