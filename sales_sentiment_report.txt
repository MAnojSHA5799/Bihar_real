
SENTIMENT BASED PLOT SALES ANALYSIS REPORT

Based on customer review analysis, total Positive Reviews: 14
Total Negative Reviews: 9

Plot sales are increasing where buyers highlight:
- Good location and amenities
- Professional and supportive staff
- Value for money pricing
- Eco-friendly and quality construction
- Timely completion of projects

These positive sentiments increase buyer trust, improve brand reputation,
and encourage word-of-mouth marketing which directly supports sales growth.

However, plot sales are getting affected due to:
- Project delays and incomplete construction
- Pending work after payment
- Refund related complaints
- Extra charges not mentioned clearly
- Poor build quality like damp walls and leakage

Negative sentiment reduces customer trust and creates hesitation among
new buyers. Financial transparency and timely delivery are critical
factors influencing purchasing decisions.

Conclusion:
Sales are strong where trust, quality, and communication are maintained.
Sales decline where delays, poor quality, and financial disputes occur.
Improving transparency, after-sale service, and construction quality
can significantly improve future plot sales performance.
