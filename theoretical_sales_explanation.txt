
================ THEORETICAL BASIS OF SALES PERFORMANCE ================

1️⃣ TRUST-BASED PURCHASE THEORY
• Real estate high-value investment hai.
• Buyer maximum risk evaluate karta hai.
• Agreement clarity aur transparency trust build karti hai.
• High perceived risk purchase intention reduce karta hai.
• Transparent documentation sales improve karti hai.

2️⃣ FINANCIAL RISK & LOSS AVERSION THEORY
• Middle-income buyers loan dependent hote hain.
• Delay se EMI + rent burden badhta hai.
• Hidden charges financial insecurity create karte hain.
• Loss Aversion Theory: Loss ka impact gain se zyada hota hai.
• Transparent pricing sales growth support karti hai.

3️⃣ SERVQUAL SERVICE QUALITY MODEL
• Reliability = On-time completion.
• Responsiveness = Quick customer support.
• Assurance = Legal clarity.
• Tangibles = Construction quality.
• Empathy = After-sale support.
• Service weakness sales ko slow karti hai.

4️⃣ ROOT CAUSES OF SALES DECLINE
• Project delays
• Incomplete work
• Refund disputes
• Hidden charges
• Quality defects

5️⃣ KEY DRIVERS OF SALES GROWTH
• Value for money pricing
• Professional team behaviour
• Eco-friendly branding
• High-quality materials
• Strong location advantage

6️⃣ CONCLUSION
High Trust + Low Risk = High Sales
Low Trust + High Risk = Sales Decline
